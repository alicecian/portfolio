2019–2021

Graduated from Carnegie Mellon Univ. with a degree in communication design and a minor in professional writing.

Created a book [15] and website [16] about critical issues in design by parsing 2700+ responses from the 2019 AIGA Design Census. Designed a mobile field guide app [17] for insect identification, and other digi-tech-citizen-science things [18]. With amazing collaborators and with other people's money*, built a nano museum [19] for her senior capstone, and fabricated a fashion-light-art-installation [20] right before the pandemic.
